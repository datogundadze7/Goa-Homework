Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 200g Pasta
  • 2 tbsp Olive oil
  • 1 Garlic clove, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

Boil the pasta in salted water. In a pan, heat olive oil and sauté garlic. Drain pasta and mix with garlic oil. Season and serve.

Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 Lettuce
  • 1 Tomato, diced
  • 1 Cucumber, sliced
  • 2 tbsp Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

Chop the vegetables and mix them in a bowl. Drizzle with olive oil, season with salt and pepper, and serve.

Chocolate Cake

Ingredients:

  • 200g Flour
  • 200g Sugar
  • 100g Cocoa powder
  • 3 Eggs
  • 100ml Milk

Instructions:

Mix all ingredients in a bowl. Pour into a baking tin and bake at 180°C for 30 minutes.
